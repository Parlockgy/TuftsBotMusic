Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 10, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your data when you use our Discord bot. By using the bot, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Data We Collect

We strive to collect the minimum amount of information required for the bot to function correctly:

  • Discord Identifiers: We may store Discord IDs (User ID, Server ID, and Channel ID) to manage permissions, preferences, and functionality within your server.
  • Command Usage: We may process command inputs and arguments to execute requested tasks (e.g., playing music).
  • Voice State Data: To provide music services, the bot accesses voice state information to connect to and disconnect from voice channels as requested by authorized users.

2. How We Use Your Data

The information collected is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To enable and maintain the bot's functionality and services.
  • To personalize your experience and remember server-specific configurations.
  • To troubleshoot technical issues and improve service reliability.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We will only disclose your data if required by law, to comply with legal processes, or to protect the safety of our users and the integrity of the service.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the data we process from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as it is necessary to provide the requested services. If you remove the bot from your server, we aim to delete server-specific configuration data in a timely manner.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Your continued use of the bot after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to the developer through our official support channels or the contact information provided on our website.
